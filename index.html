What is GEO, generative engine optimisation? +
GEO is the practice of structuring content so that AI-powered search engines, including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews, retrieve and cite it directly in generated answers. It builds on SEO fundamentals but optimises for citation inside an AI response rather than a ranked link.
Who is this for? +
Whether you are a DeFi protocol, a SaaS startup, or a brand tired of watching competitors get cited instead of you, the work is the same: structure content so search engines and AI systems find you first.

No. The SEO and GEO fundamentals apply to any industry. Crypto is where I built the deepest expertise because the audience is unusually skeptical and fast-moving, which forced sharper content.

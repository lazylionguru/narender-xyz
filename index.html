What is GEO, generative engine optimisation? +
GEO is the practice of structuring content so that AI-powered search engines — Perplexity, ChatGPT search, and Google AI Overviews — retrieve and cite your content in generated answers. Unlike traditional SEO which targets rankings, GEO targets citations. It combines entity clarity, FAQ structure, authoritative signals, and concise factual writing so that when an AI summarises a topic, your brand is the answer it gives.

What does a GEO content audit involve? +
A GEO audit assesses how well your existing content is structured for retrieval by AI systems. It covers entity definition clarity, FAQ schema presence, citation-worthy factual writing, content conciseness, and whether your brand currently appears in AI-generated answers for relevant queries. The output is a prioritised action plan to improve AI visibility alongside traditional search rankings.
